About ResetSecureLLC

Friendly tech support designed with seniors in mind.

Our Story

ResetSecureLLC was founded in 2015 by Michael Johnson after he spent countless hours helping his own parents with their technology challenges. He realized that most tech support services weren't designed with seniors in mind - they moved too fast, used confusing jargon, and didn't have the patience needed.

We set out to create a different kind of tech support company - one that truly understands the needs of older adults. Our team is trained to be patient, speak in plain language, and never make you feel rushed or embarrassed about your questions.

Our Mission

"To empower seniors with the confidence and skills to use technology safely and effectively, while treating every customer with the patience and respect they deserve."
